Our Core Programs

Objective

To provide rural schools with access to quality learning materials and safe reading spaces.

Activities

  • Establishing libraries in partner schools
  • Supplying books and educational resources
  • Training school librarians

Objective

To enhance teaching quality through ongoing professional development.

Activities

  • Workshops on modern pedagogy
  • Peer learning and mentorship
  • Resource sharing sessions

Objective

To equip students and teachers with essential digital skills for the 21st century.

Activities

  • ICT training workshops
  • Provision of computers and tablets
  • Internet safety education

Objective

To mobilize communities for education advocacy and sustainable development.

Activities

  • Community sensitization meetings
  • Parent engagement forums
  • Advocacy campaigns
