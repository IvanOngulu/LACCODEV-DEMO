About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018, LACCODEV was born out of a passion to address the educational and social challenges facing children in Northern Uganda. Our founders, educators and community leaders from the Lango sub-region, saw the urgent need for resources, teacher support, and community-driven change.

Our Vision

A literate, empowered, and prosperous Lango community where every child thrives.

Our Mission

To transform education and opportunity in Northern Uganda through innovative programs, resources, and advocacy.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency.
  • Empowerment: We build capacity and confidence in our communities.
  • Inclusivity: We serve all, regardless of background or ability.
  • Collaboration: We work together for greater impact.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas for lasting change.
